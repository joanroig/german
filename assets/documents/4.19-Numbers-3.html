Ordinal numbers

German ordinal numbers are pretty regular. The general rule is:

number range ending
1-19 -te
> 19 -ste
Irregular forms
1. erste
3. dritte
7. siebte

Ordinal numbers behave like adjectives, so their endings will change accordingly:
